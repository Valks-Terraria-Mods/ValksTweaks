This mod allows you to configure the attack speed of most weapons.

FAQ:
Q: The mod does not work?
A: Make sure you are either using a ranged or magic type weapon and you have configured the attack speed slider to a higher value.

Known Issues:
- Melee weapons are not affected
- Thrown weapons may not work

Source: https://github.com/Valks-Terraria-Mods/FasterDamages (all contributions welcome)
